Data frame of daily temperatures in Hilo, HI and Death Valley, CA

Description:

     These data consist of daily average, min, and max temperatures in Hilo, HI and Death Valley, CA from 01/01/2000 until

Format:

     Data frame has 136 observations on the following 4 variables.

     'STATION' ID of the weather station

     'NAME' Name of the weather station

     'DATE' The date on which the observation was collected

     'TAVG' The daily average temperature in F

     'TMAX' The daily maximum temperature in F
     
     'TMIN' The daily minimum temperature in F

Source:

     https://www.ncdc.noaa.gov/cdo-web/
