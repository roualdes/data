Data frame of daily temperatures in Hilo, HI and Death Valley, CA

Description:

     These data consist of daily average, min, and max temperatures in Hilo, HI and Death Valley, CA from 01/01/2000 until

Format:

     'date' The date on which the observation was collected

     'mintempHilo' The daily minimum temperature (F) in Hilo

     'mintempDV' The daily minimum temperature (F) in Death Valley
